About Us

HG Co., Ltd. specializes in the distribution of high-quality telecommunications products in Japan, including SoftBank Hikari, SoftBank Air, Pocket Wi-Fi, and SIM Cards. Established on August 5, 2024, our company is headquartered in Room 60, 6th Floor, Binh Thuy Building, No. 26 Phan Dinh Phung Street, South Ward Ha, Ha Tinh City, Ha Tinh Province, Vietnam, with a branch office located at 〒169-0073, 4-5-15 Hyakunincho, Shinjuku, Tokyo, Japan.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to providing reliable, high-quality internet services to connect the immigrant community in Japan. Our mission is to make information access seamless, help people stay connected with their families, and empower them to build a strong future.

In the next 10 years, HG Co., Ltd.aspires to become the leading internet service provider trusted by immigrants in Japan. We aim to achieve this through unmatched convenience, superior support, and premium-quality services.

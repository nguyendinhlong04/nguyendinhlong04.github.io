About Us

UP BOOST Co., Ltd. specializes in the distribution of high-quality telecommunications products in Japan, including SoftBank Hikari, SoftBank Air, Pocket Wi-Fi, and SIM Cards. Established on May 4, 2023, our company is headquartered in Bình Vinh Village, Cẩm Bình Commune, Cẩm Xuyên District, Hà Tĩnh Province, Vietnam, with a branch office located at 〒169-0073, 4-5-15 Hyakunincho, Shinjuku, Tokyo, Japan.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to providing reliable, high-quality internet services to connect the immigrant community in Japan. Our mission is to make information access seamless, help people stay connected with their families, and empower them to build a strong future.

Our Vision

In the next 10 years, UP BOOST Co., Ltd. aspires to become the leading internet service provider trusted by immigrants in Japan. We aim to achieve this through unmatched convenience, superior support, and premium-quality services.
